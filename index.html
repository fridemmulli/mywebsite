Hello i'm Fridem

Hello! I'm Fridem Mulli, a student currently pursuing my education at Universitas Advent Indonesia in the Information Systems department. I'm currently in my second semester and am striving to complete my studies while working to meet my educational needs.

I believe that difficulties are stepping stones to success, and each challenge only makes me stronger and more prepared to face the professional world in the future.
